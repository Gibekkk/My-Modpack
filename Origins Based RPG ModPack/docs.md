# Origins Based ModPack
## Introduction
This ModPack was created to provide a more immersive multiplayer experience. The main goal is to explore the map by working together and forming partnerships, or going hardcore and beating the game solo through a much more difficult and challenging path

## Mods
### Origins
This mod focuses on providing rewards at a certain "cost," depending on the impact of the buff, featuring up to 3 tiers. The created Origins are built from several buff and debuff modules that balance each other out within each Origin. The costs or trade-offs in this mod are divided into 5 categories:

* **Biome Debuffs:** Debuffs that prevent players from entering specific biomes by dealing damage or killing the player upon entering certain areas (e.g., taking damage when touching water).
* **Equipment Debuffs:** Debuffs that restrict players from using certain types of equipment or reduce their efficiency (e.g., reduced sword damage or the inability to use an Elytra).
* **Effect / Removal of Effect Debuffs:** Debuffs that apply negative status effects such as Hunger or Blindness, or strip away the effects of a buff (e.g., negating health regeneration).
* **Attribute Debuffs:** Debuffs that cause players to lose an ability or reduce its effectiveness (e.g., reduced maximum HP or taking increased damage from specific mobs).
* **Miscellaneous Debuffs:** Debuffs stemming from other factors, such as requiring significant game progression or specific items just to unlock or maintain the Origin's effects.

The Origins in this ModPack are divided into 4 categories:

* [Base Origins](https://origins-mod.fandom.com/wiki/Origins)
* [Extra Origins](https://www.9minecraft.net/extra-origins-mod/wiki)
* [Origins++](https://github.com/kgorins/origins-plus-plus-wiki)
* [Dig's DnD Origins](https://www.modpackindex.com/mod/44249/digs-dnd-origins)

To make switching easier, this ModPack also includes [DarkOrb](https://modrinth.com/mod/darkorb), which simplifies the crafting process for the *Orb of Origin* used to change your Origin.

### Classes (RPG Series)
This mod introduces several classes that can be utilized at the cost of EXP. In addition to class-specific skills, there is also dedicated equipment for each class and accessories like [Jewelry](https://modrinth.com/mod/jewelry). The mod requires players to find books to craft into spellbooks using a Spell Binding Table, which can be found in village [Gazebos](https://modrinth.com/mod/gazebos). The Spell Binding Table works similarly to an Enchanting Table—it uses bookshelves to increase its power and consumes EXP to bind new spells into the book.

In this ModPack, there are several playable classes available:

* [Wizard](https://www.minecraft-guides.com/mod/wizards/)
* [Archer](https://modrinth.com/mod/archers)
* [Paladin and Priest](https://modrinth.com/mod/paladins-and-priests)
* [Rogue and Warrior](https://modrinth.com/mod/rogues-and-warriors)
* [Death Knight](https://modrinth.com/mod/death-knights)

### Food & Splice of Life
In this ModPack, there are also various foods that can be crafted from different biomes, as well as buffs that can be obtained by maintaining a good and healthy diet through [Spice of Life](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/spice-of-fabric) (Spice of Fabric). Here are some of the food expansions available in this ModPack:

* [Farmer's Delight](https://farmersdelight.fandom.com/wiki/Farmer%27s_Delight_Wiki)
* [Farmer's Respite](https://modrinth.com/mod/farmers-respite)
* [Nether's Delight](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/nethers-delight-fabric)
* [End's Delight](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/ends-delight)
* [Ocean's Delight](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/oceans-delight)
* [Chef's Delight](https://modrinth.com/mod/chefs-delight)

Additionally, this ModPack includes [Display Delight](https://modrinth.com/mod/display-delight-fabric), which allows players to place food down as blocks so it can be shared and enjoyed together.

### Looting and Storage
In this ModPack, there is also the [Backpack](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/backpacked-fabric) mod, allowing you to carry extra inventory slots wherever you go, and [Sophisticated Storage](https://modrinth.com/mod/sophisticated-storage) to add more variety to the vanilla storage system. Additionally, this ModPack features [Lootr](https://modrinth.com/mod/lootr), which generates instanced loot so every player gets their own share of rewards during exploration! Finally, if you still need more storage flexibility, [Carry-On](https://modrinth.com/mod/carry-on) allows you to simply pick up chests with your bare hands and carry them away!

### Transportation
This ModPack features many new structures across every biome, including up in the sky and out in the ocean! To make transportation easier, it includes [Immersive Aircraft](https://www.minecraft-guides.com/mod/immersive-aircraft) and [Small Ships](https://www.minecraft-guides.com/mod/small-ships)!

### Utilites & Diversities
In this ModPack, the combat system is enhanced with [Better Combat](https://modrinth.com/mod/better-combat) to add variety to your attacks, and [Combat Roll](https://modrinth.com/mod/combat-roll) to increase the intensity of battles! Additionally, there is a wide array of extra equipment to expand your fighting styles from [Epic Knight](https://www.modpackindex.com/mod/27373/epic-knights-shields-armor-and-weapons-fabricforgeneoforge) and [Simply Swords](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/simply-swords), as well as [Better Tridents](https://modrinth.com/mod/better-tridents)! The ModPack is also equipped with dynamic animations using [Punchy!](https://modrinth.com/mod/punchy-fpa).

To make it easier to keep track of partners and allies, this ModPack features [Heartstones](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/heartstone) that you can give to your companions. To ease your progression and ease the punishment of dying, there is also the [Grave](https://modrinth.com/mod/graves) mod, which safely stores your items before they completely despawn.

However, to provide a fresh challenge to vanilla survival, we've included [Dungeons Difficulty](https://modrinth.com/mod/dungeon-difficulty), which progressively increases the difficulty with every passing in-game day; [Sleep Tight](https://modrinth.com/mod/sleep-tight), which introduces both rewards and new challenges for a successful night's sleep; and [Enhanced Celestials](https://modrinth.com/mod/enhanced-celestials), which triggers special lunar events that offer either blessings or dangerous challenges each night.

Beyond all this, the ModPack includes several other self-explanatory mods that are incredibly fun to discover and explore on your own while playing alongside your friends!
